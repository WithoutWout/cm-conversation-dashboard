How to Get Export Files

Follow these steps in the CM.com AI Cloud dashboard to download the JSON export files needed by this app.

Articles

1 In CM.com AI Cloud, open the Articles section from the left navigation.
2 Click Export in the top toolbar or action menu.
3 Choose Export JSON and save the downloaded file.
4 Back in Settings, click Update Articles file… and select the downloaded file.

Dialogs

1 In CM.com AI Cloud, open the Dialogs section from the left navigation.
2 Click Export in the top toolbar or action menu.
3 Choose Export JSON and save the downloaded file.
4 Back in Settings, click Update Dialogs file… and select the downloaded file.
