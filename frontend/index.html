How to Get Export Files

Follow these steps in the CM.com AI Cloud dashboard to download the JSON export files needed by this app.

Articles

1 In CM.com Conversational AI Cloud, open the Articles section from the left navigation.
2 Click the three dots menu and click Export.
3 Choose Export JSON and save the downloaded file.
4 Save the downloaded file in the folder you selected in this app under Settings → Data Folder.

Dialogs

1 In CM.com Conversational AI Cloud, open the Dialogs section from the left navigation.
2 Click the three dots menu and click Export.
3 Choose Export JSON and save the downloaded file.
4 Save the downloaded file in the folder you selected in this app under Settings → Data Folder.

Entities

1 In CM.com Conversational AI Cloud, open the Entities section from the left navigation.
2 Click the three dots menu and select Export.
3 Save the downloaded .csv file in the same data folder you selected in this app.

How to Export the Interaction Log

The Interaction Log is exported one day at a time from the Analytics section. Large exports may take a moment to generate.

Steps

1 In CM.com Conversational AI Cloud, open Analytics from the left navigation.
2 Click Interactions Export.
3 Select the day you want to export using the date picker.
4 Make sure CSV is selected as the export format.
5 Click Export and wait for the download to complete. Large days may take a while.
6 In the Conversations tab of this app, use the Import CSV button to load the downloaded file.
⚠  Only one day at a time can be exported. Repeat the steps above for each day you need.
